# Primary Audience

The app will primarily serve two key user groups:

## Volunteers

Individuals who dedicate their time to rescue organizations, assisting with dog care, events, and other operational tasks. They will need tools to manage their schedules, view available tasks, and communicate.

## People Looking to Adopt

Individuals and families actively searching for a dog to adopt. They will need features to browse available dogs, filter results, view detailed profiles, and initiate the adoption process.
